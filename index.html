Our Services

At AVA House, we take the time to understand each young person's journey and aspirations. We provide the right support, guidance, and environment to prepare them for independence — not the other way around. Backed by trauma-informed practice, cultural respect, and a commitment to real outcomes, AVA House is proud to support statutory care, NDIS, and Justice-funded young people across Queensland.

Community Access

Get support to attend appointments, explore hobbies, visit friends, or just get out and about. We're here to make your world more connected.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

We provide 24/7 support in shared or individual living environments. Our SIL homes are safe, welcoming and staffed by experienced workers.

In-Home Support

Respectful, reliable help with personal care, daily routines, household tasks, and more — all in the comfort of your home.

Daily Living & Life Skills

We help you build confidence and independence — from travel training and budgeting to cooking and self-care routines.

Our Care Model

At AVA House, our care model is built on the belief that every young person deserves to heal, grow, and thrive in a supportive, culturally grounded environment. Our holistic framework blends Cultural, Spiritual, Emotional, Practical, and Educational care — nurturing identity, faith, resilience, and independence.
